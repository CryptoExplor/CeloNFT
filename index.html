❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to mint?

The current mint price is loading... CELO plus gas fees (usually ~$0.01-0.02). The mint price is set by the contract owner.

What makes each NFT unique?

Each NFT captures the live CELO price at the exact moment of minting! The rarity is determined by the price - rare prices get legendary or mythic ratings. Your NFT is a snapshot of crypto history!

How does rarity work?

Rarity is based on the CELO price when you mint:
• Common: Most frequent prices
• Rare: Unusual price ranges
• Legendary: Very rare prices
• Mythic: Extremely rare, historic prices

Can I sell or transfer my NFT?

Yes! Your NFT is a standard ERC-721 token on Celo blockchain. You can trade it on any NFT marketplace that supports Celo, transfer it to other wallets, or hold it as a collectible.

What network is this on?

This NFT collection is on Celo Mainnet. Make sure your wallet is connected to Celo network. Gas fees are very low (usually under $0.02)!

How many can I mint?

You can mint as many as you want! Each mint captures the current CELO price, so minting at different times gives you different NFTs. Watch the price chart and mint when you see an interesting price!

What wallets are supported?

We support all major Web3 wallets including MetaMask, Valora (Celo's native wallet), Rainbow, Coinbase Wallet, WalletConnect, and more through Reown AppKit.

Is this on Farcaster?

Yes! You can mint directly in the Farcaster miniapp or on our standalone website. Your NFTs are the same on both - they're all on Celo blockchain.

Can I download my NFT?

Absolutely! After minting, use the download buttons to save your NFT as SVG (vector), PNG (image), or JSON (metadata). Your NFT is fully on-chain, so you truly own it!

What happens if minting fails?

Common issues:
• Not enough CELO: Add funds to your wallet
• Wrong network: Switch to Celo Mainnet
• Gas too low: Increase gas limit
• Transaction stuck: Check your wallet for pending transactions
